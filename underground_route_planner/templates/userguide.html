User Guide

How to use the London Underground Planner

Selecting Your Route In Four Easy Steps!

  1. Firstly, enter your origin location.
  2. Next, enter your desired destination.
  3. Similarly, enter the time you wish to depart.
  4. Lastly, click the search button.

Step 1.

Selecting Your Origin

To start, you first need to type your starting point into the "From" input box.

Once you start typing, a list of suggested stations will appear. You can click one of these to auto input it into the field.

If your desired station isn't recommended, you can continue typing the full station name until it appears.

Step 2.

Selecting Your Destination

To start, you first need to type your destination point into the "To" input box.

Once you start typing, a list of suggested stations will appear. You can click one of these to auto input it into the field.

If your desired station isn't recommended, you can continue typing the full station name until it appears.

Step 3.

Selecting Your Departure Time

Next, you will need to enter your time of departure into the "At" input box

Step 4.

Searching For Your Route

Finally, click search. It really is that simple!
